Grilled Cheese Recipe

Learn how to make some delicious Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Turn the stove on
  2. Spread margarine on one side of each slice of bread
  3. Place one slice of bread on the stove with the margarine touching the pan
  4. Place cheese on bread
  5. Place the second slice of bread with the margarine side up
  6. Cook on the pan until there is crisp to your liking
  7. Enjoy!
